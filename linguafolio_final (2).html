Our Story

Born from a Love of Language & Literature

LinguaFolio was founded in 2019 by a team of linguists, teachers, and passionate readers who believed that the right book, at the right level, could transform how anyone learns a language.

Our Mission

We exist to make world-class language learning books accessible to every curious mind — regardless of where they live or what they can afford. We partner with publishers across 20 countries to bring you books that are pedagogically sound, culturally rich, and genuinely enjoyable to read.

Our Vision

A world where language barriers no longer limit human connection. We believe that learning even the basics of another language opens doors to friendships, opportunities, and perspectives that transform lives. One book at a time, we're building that world.

What We Stand For

Our Values

📖

Depth over Gimmicks

We believe real language learning requires depth and patience. We curate books that build true understanding, not just surface phrases.

🌍

Cultural Respect

Every language carries a culture. Our books honour the heritage and context of each language, not just its grammar.

🤝

Accessible to All

Quality education should not be a luxury. We offer fair pricing, regional discounts, and a growing free library for beginners.
